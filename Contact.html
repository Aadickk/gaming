About us.

AITU Gaming is a community whose principles are to increase interest in the esports industry by young people (applicants and students). The main characteristics of the club are considered to be student leadership, interest and involvement in the esports industry, participation in esports competitions. The administrative staff of the gaming club has developed this document in order to provide the university management with the structural basis necessary for the club to receive support, develop leadership skills and hold events of various scales.
